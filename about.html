Welcome to Green Field Public school

Green Field Public School Sihunta is situated amidst the serene landscapes of district Chamba. Established in 1994, it holds the distinction of being one of the oldest and renowned co-educational institutions in Bhattiyat. Managed by The Green Field Public School Sihunta Educational Society, the school is dedicated to providing elementary and higher education to its students.

Affiliated with the Himachal Pradesh Board of School Education since its inception, the school is committed to offering the best educational facilities, fostering the complete development of its students. The school's guiding motto is "NOT ME BUT YOU." The Department of Education of Himachal Pradesh has issued a No Objection Certificate (NOC No. EDN-II 21 A(1)P S-2/2007CMB-NOC dated 27-02-2008), and the school is officially recognized by the Department of Education of Himachal Pradesh.

Over the years, Green Field Public School Sihunta has produced meritorious students who have contributed significantly to serving society. Students come to learn and leave with a commitment to serve. In 2015, Sh. Virbhadra Singh, Honourable Chief Minister of Himachal Pradesh, honored our student Mohit Singh, who stood 13th in the board examination of Himachal Pradesh.

Vision:

Our vision is to impart quality-based education to the students, empowering them to be well-informed, nationally conscious, and to embody the motto "NOT ME BUT YOU" for global development.
